Handling Missing Data for Olympus Manufacturing

Scenario

Olympus Manufacturing, a UK-based company, operates two factories that produce industrial components for the automotive, construction, and consumer goods industries. Their manufacturing process involves sourcing raw materials, assembling components, conducting quality control checks, and distributing products to suppliers and businesses.

Recently, data analysts at Olympus Manufacturing have identified issues with missing data in their inventory, production, and distribution records. These gaps are creating inefficiencies in supply chain management, production scheduling, and quality assurance. Your task is to identify, summarise, visualise, and handle the missing data in the dataset provided.

Task: Detecting and Visualising Missing Data

You have been given a sample dataset (olympus_manufacturing_production.xlsx), which includes:

  • Inventory Levels: Records of raw material availability at both factory locations.
  • Production Data: Details of component assembly, including time taken and materials used.
  • Quality Control: Inspection reports indicating whether components meet manufacturing standards.
  • Distribution Records: Shipments from factories to suppliers and businesses.

However, some key data points are missing, affecting:

  • Stock levels in certain raw materials, leading to production delays.
  • Incomplete production data, making it difficult to track assembly efficiency.
  • Missing quality control records, creating uncertainty about product defects.
  • Distribution gaps, where some shipments lack delivery status updates.

  • Identify missing values and summarise them (count & percentage).
