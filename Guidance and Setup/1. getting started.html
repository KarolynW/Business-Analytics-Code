Welcome to Business Analytics

Introduction

Welcome to the Foundations of Business Analytics module! This course introduces the core concepts and techniques that underpin modern business analytics.

Throughout the course, you will learn how to approach data-driven decision-making and explore practical tools for analysing and interpreting data effectively. The aim is to empower you with the skills to apply analytics in real-world business scenarios.

Course Structure

The course is structured to gradually build your knowledge, starting with foundational concepts and progressing to advanced techniques. You will engage with a mix of theoretical insights, practical coding exercises, and case studies designed to simulate real-world business challenges.

By the end of the course, you will have developed key competencies in handling data, performing analyses, and presenting insights using modern tools and programming languages.

Programming Languages: Python and R

This course uses two powerful programming languages: Python and R. Each has unique strengths that make them indispensable for business analytics:

  • Python: A versatile and beginner-friendly programming language widely used in the industry for data analysis, machine learning, and automation.
  • R: A language dedicated to statistical computing and data visualisation, known for its powerful analytical capabilities and graphing tools.

Although both are suitable for data analysis, the focus of each language differs:

Python R
Excellent for scripting, automation, and building data applications. Excels in statistical analysis and creating publication-quality visualisations.
Broad range of libraries, including machine learning tools like scikit-learn. Comprehensive packages for statistical techniques, such as ggplot2 for visualisation.
Widely adopted in various industries, from tech to finance. Preferred by researchers and academic professionals for its statistical focus.
